# Madrid recommendations
### Food 🤤
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/QXXbwtdarUk44vh78 - Tacos
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/np1df8dmojFx2EGH7 - Tacos (Its a chain so you can search Tiki Taco and check nearest place)
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/DTGcc1AunE3kC8hh8 - Tacos (Maybe you will need to book a table .. once you inside you can order without queue).
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/Ff4gaoUHjtNcZW7R6 - Burritos (its a chain so search for Tierra Burrito).
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/f7bYukDvoEfHSZ9u5 - Senegalese food
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/4h68aKoM1NxrEb6c6 - Spanish, a little bit of everything. Caralladas, Octopus, Torrezno, Chuleton.
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/X8X4FrKxDEuCJ6819 - Italian
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/qZM2AANfrB1bMEw46 - Argentinian pizza, go here if you love mozzarella. Try the fugazzetta (but you'll 4 people per pie).
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/tPuNvvj1Z3C8V4RH8 - Fancy italian inspired, my GF's favorite
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/GnL7dsdfsDEXZ1ya6 - Fusion tapas place, my favorite. Get the tasting menu.
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/WQAVVm1tZTsofSot7 - Spanish, a little bit of everything.  Pretty much anything in the "para picar y disfrutar" section is amazing
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/FmfCkWXDuNW5EDaU7 - Michelin list place, fancy experimental mix of asian and spanish. Get the electrifying candy.
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/jQRTxcVhD9Ef1QJp9 - Tortilla, gets packed so you need to go real early when it opens, eat a piece and get out
- https://maps.app.goo.gl/5W4395nCDNK8mBCp6 - Spanish, Asturias. Try cabrales cheese and cachopos
